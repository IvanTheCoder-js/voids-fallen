Waves Strategy Placement Map
Notes
  • Skip ONLY 1 wave. Others are not reccomended but optional
  • You can also replace militant with commander! It won't change much
0 - 21
  • Waves 1-4 : Place Electroshocker in E1 position and upgrade it to level 2
  • Waves 4-10 : Place 3 level 3 Military bases 1 by 1
  • Waves 10-11 : Upgrade Electroshocker to level 3
  • Waves 11-18 : Upgrade all 3 Military bases to level 4 1 by 1( Don't use airstrike as it may ruin whole economy )
  • Waves 18-21 : Place 2 more level 4 Military bases 1 by 1
21 - 40
  • Waves 21-22 : Place 3 more level 0 Electroshockers
  • Waves 22-24 : Upgrade your level 3 Electroshocker to level 4, and then upgrade every other Electroshocker to level 4 1 by 1
  • Wave 24 : Place a level 3 Dj (Set him on green track and spam his ability)
  • Waves 25-29 : Max all your Military bases 1 by 1
  • Waves 29-30 : Max all your Electroshockers 1 by 1
  • Waves 30-31 : Max dj
  • Waves 31-33 : Place 7 level 2 Rangers
  • Waves 33-36 : Max all your Rangers 1 by 1
  • Wave 36 : Set Dj to red track, and use ability when necessary
  • Waves 36-39 : Spam max level Militants 1 by 1 (If you use Commander, place 4 maxed Commanders 1 by 1 and chain CoA[Call of Arms])
  • Wave 40 : Spam Dj Ability when fallen king is in range(If you are using Commander, then also spam APC's and CoA)
